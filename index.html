About

Based in Cwmbran, South Wales, Gareth Prosser is an experienced construction specialist, with over 20 years in the building industry.

Specialising in brickwork, driveways and extensions, Gareth provides a professional, reliable service from consultation to completion.

Through word of mouth and recommendation, Gareth has gained a strong reputation for quality craftsmanship, expertise and reliability.

His extensive knowledge of construction enables him to provide sound advice as well as a unique creative insight, turning his clients’ visions into reality and managing projects from conception through to completion.

He works regularly with a highly skilled and trusted network of contractors and suppliers, providing a comprehensive range of specialist services while maintaining competitive costs. This includes all aspects of carpentry, plumbing, electrics and plastering.

Throughout his career, Gareth has established an unrivalled attention to detail and a renowned expertise, striving for perfection in every project he completes. From creating bespoke proposals, to sourcing the highest quality materials, he takes a deep pride in his work that is appreciated by both clients and fellow tradesmen alike.
